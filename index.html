About SimLauncher

SimLauncher is a research project focused on sample-efficient robotic reinforcement learning through simulation pre-training.

This website demonstrates our approach with:

  • Video demonstrations of our method
  • Visual examples of simulation-to-real transfer
  • Research findings and results
